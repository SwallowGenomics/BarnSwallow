To extract intergenic regions coordinates use:

bedtools merge -i genes.bed > genes_merged.bed  #merge genes coordinates
samtools faidx bHirRus1.fasta #index the reference genome
cut -f1,2 bHirRus1.fasta.fai > bHirRus1_sizes.txt #extract scaffold sizes
bedtools sort -i bHirRus1_sizes.txt > bHirRus1_sizes_sorted.txt #sort sizes
bedtools sort -i genes_merged.bed > genes_merged_sorted.bed #sort genes
bedtools complement -i genes_merged_sorted.bed -g bHirRus1_sizes_sorted.txt > intergenic.bed
